About Us：

History:

We have opened this pet stores for more than four years and our store founder is Christina Parke, who is also the marketing manager now. In the beginning, we were a small store that located in the 38 Pets street, close to University of New Hampshire. As we become more and more popular, we enlarge our physical store and started open online shopping service for our customer two years ago.

Why use us?

Keep your pet clean and healthy with dog grooming and self-service pet wash available in our store. We provide delivery service for our customer, you can simply give us a call and we will provide free shipping in one business day to our customer. Also, bring your dog or cat into our store, save on the cost of vaccinations, heartworm and lyme disease testing and purchase prescription flea, tick and heartworm prevention products.
